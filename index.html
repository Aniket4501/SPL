About the Challenge

Steps Premier League (SPL) is a 5-day step challenge for HCL Healthcare employees. Each day you walk, score cricket-style runs, and help your team compete for glory. Runs are earned based on step goals, streaks, and team performance. The team with the highest cumulative runs at the end wins!

5 Days Challenge

How It Works

1

Setup

Admin/HR defines daily step goal and challenge dates

2

Team Formation

Teams are created with 5 players each, Captain appointed by Admin/HR

3

Registration

Players are registered into teams (no self-creation)

4

Daily Play

Each day = 1 Over. Steps synced via Habit Health app

5

Scoring

Steps converted into cricket runs based on scoring system

6

Results

Daily leaderboard published + Final winners announced
