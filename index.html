Where Fitness Meets Cricket Excellence

Steps Premier League (SPL) is a revolutionary 7-day fitness challenge that transforms your daily walks into an exciting cricket championship. Designed exclusively for HCL Healthcare employees, this gamified wellness program makes fitness fun, competitive, and rewarding.
