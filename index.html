About

The Ultimate Fitness Challenge

Steps Premier League (SPL) is a 7-day fitness challenge designed exclusively for HCL Healthcare employees. Walk daily, achieve step goals, and help your team score cricket-style runs. The team with the highest total wins!

🏏 7 Days = 7 Overs of Pure Competition
Process

How It Works

Six simple steps to championship glory

1

Challenge Setup

Admin/HR sets the daily step goal (8,000 steps) and defines challenge dates.

2

Team Formation

Players are grouped into teams of 5, with a Captain appointed by Admin/HR.

3

Registration

Players are registered into teams and join the StepUp Showdown challenge on Habit Health app.

4

Daily Play

Each day = 1 Over. Players must sync their steps daily in the Habit Health app.

5

Scoring & Results

Steps convert to cricket runs. Leaderboard updates daily (not real-time).

6

Communication

Join WhatsApp Community. Captains guide & motivate teams to victory.
