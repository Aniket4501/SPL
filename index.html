Steps Premier League

Walk. Score. Win. Let's play fitness cricket together!

About the Challenge

"Steps Premier League (SPL) is a 7-day fitness challenge designed exclusively for HCL Healthcare employees. Walk daily, achieve step goals, and help your team score cricket-style runs. The team with the highest total wins!"

🏏 7 Days (Overs) Challenge

How It Works

1

Challenge Setup

Admin/HR sets the daily step goal (8,000 steps) and defines challenge dates.

2

Team Formation

Players are grouped into teams of 5, with a Captain appointed by Admin/HR.

3

Registration

Players are registered into teams by Admin/HR and must also join the StepUp Showdown challenge on the Habit Health app for step tracking.

4

Daily Play

Each day = 1 Over. Players must sync their steps daily in the Habit Health app.

5

Scoring & Results

Steps are converted into cricket runs as per the scoring rules. The leaderboard is updated daily (not real-time) and shared with all participants.

6

Communication & Roles

All players join the WhatsApp Community. Each team has a Captain-led group, announcements come from Admin, and Captains should guide & motivate their teams.

Game Rules & Scoring

🎯 Daily Goal

8,000 steps per day for all players

⚡ Power Play Day

Day 4 = Power Play
All runs are doubled (×2)

🏃 Runs Scoring System

0-4,000
0-50% Goal
0 Runs
4,001-8,000
50-100% Goal
1 Run
8,001-12,000
100-150% Goal
4 Runs
12,001-16,000
150-200% Goal
6 Runs

🏏 Half Century Bonus (Day 3)

Team Level Bonus: Total Bonus Runs at Day 3 = 50 Runs

Example: If all players of Team A maintain a 3-day streak, then Team A is awarded +50 Runs.

💯 Century Bonus (Day 7)

Team Level Bonus: Total Bonus Runs at Day 7 = 100 Runs

Example: If all players of Team A maintain a 7-day streak, then Team A is awarded +100 Runs.

⚡ Power Play Day (Day 4)

All runs are doubled (×2) for Day 4 only.
